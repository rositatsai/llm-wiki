# Australian Digital Health Agency (ADHA)

**國家：** Australia
**性質：** 澳洲聯邦政府數位健康機構

## 主要貢獻

- **National Nursing and Midwifery Digital Health Capability Framework (2020)**（與 AIDH 合作）
- **National Digital Health Capability Action Plan (2020)**
- **National Digital Health Workforce and Education Roadmap (2020)**
- 澳洲在全球數位健康能力框架領域最為完整，ADHA 是核心推動機構
