# Steven Lin

**身份：** MD, Physician
**機構：** Stanford Healthcare AI Applied Research Team, Division of Primary Care and Population Health, Stanford University School of Medicine
**研究領域：** 基層醫療 AI 導入、臨床文件 AI、品質改善方法

## 主要著作與貢獻

- **A Clinician's Guide to AI: Why and How Primary Care Should Lead the Health Care AI Revolution** — *JABFM* 2022;35:175–184. 主張基層醫療最適合領導醫療 AI 革命
- **Ten ways AI will transform primary care** — *J Gen Intern Med* 2019;34:1626–30
- **From code to bedside: implementing AI using QI methods**（合著）— *J Gen Intern Med* 2021;36:1061–6. 提出 The Stanford Method 六步驟 AI 導入方法
- **Reimagining clinical documentation with AI** — *Mayo Clin Proc* 2018;93:563–5
- **The present and future of team documentation** — *Mayo Clin Proc* 2020;95:852–5
